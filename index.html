Privacy Policy

KrepsCo — Internal Advertising Tools

About This Application

This application is an internal tool operated by KrepsCo, a digital advertising agency. It is used by KrepsCo employees to access and review advertising campaign data on behalf of our clients whose accounts we manage.

Data We Access

This application accesses advertising and business management data through the Meta Platform API, including ad campaign performance metrics and account information. This data already exists within our clients' Meta accounts — we do not create, collect, or generate any additional data.

How We Use Data

Data accessed through the Meta API is used solely for managing and reporting on advertising campaigns on behalf of our clients. We do not:

  • Collect personal data from end users
  • Sell or share data with third parties
  • Use data for purposes beyond campaign management and reporting
  • Use tracking pixels or collect data from external websites

Data Storage and Security

Data retrieved from the Meta API is processed in real time for reporting purposes. Access to the application is restricted to authorized KrepsCo employees. We maintain appropriate security measures to protect any data handled by the application.

Data Retention

We do not maintain a persistent store of data retrieved from the Meta API beyond what is necessary for generating reports. Campaign data remains in our clients' Meta accounts and is subject to Meta's own data retention policies.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@kreps.co.
